Coca-Cola HBC

During my Master's degree project, we conducted a thorough analysis of Coca-Cola HBC's beverage portfolio to develop effective positioning strategies for each brand. We examined how various products were perceived in the market, focusing on mainstream products to strengthen emotional connections and on health-oriented products to highlight their benefits. To maximize brand visibility and consumer engagement, we integrated both Above The Line (ATL) and Below The Line (BTL) marketing strategies, such as national advertising campaigns and direct promotional activities.
